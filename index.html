    This is
    Elkhart Republic

    A brand-new, first-class, supporter-owned, cerveza & fruit smoothie-fueled soccer club with steel roots in Elkhart Indiana.

    We'll be crushing skulls in the Premier League of America (tier-4 of the U.S. Soccer Pyramid), with our first league game in March 2017. But we've got plenty on the dockett over the next few months too: tryouts, friendlies, cocktail hours, and kit reveals to start. The best thing to do now is gear up for a history-making season, take a swig of our delicious ale, learn a chant or two, study up on our (alternate) history, and most importantly — however you want to do it — join the club:
